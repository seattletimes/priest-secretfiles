Assignments of a pedophile priest

The Seattle Archdiocese transferred Father Michael Cody to several unsuspecting parishes in Western Washington in the 1960s and ‘70s despite knowing he was a pedophile. Cody later admitted to abusing up to 41 children over 20 years, though the actual number of his victims isn’t clear. At least 19 known or alleged victims of Cody are accounted for in public records, including 10 people who’ve claimed in lawsuits Cody abused them years after Archbishop Thomas Connolly knew about Cody’s sexual deviancy.
